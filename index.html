Orange Juice Cake

Gudenzi's Famous Orange Juice Cake

  • Yellow cake mix
  • Orange juice
  • Vegetable oil
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Powdered sugar
  1. Heat oven to 325Â°F for Bundt pan. Grease inside of the pan.
  2. Mix Cake Mix, 1/2 cup of water, 1/2 cup of orange juice, 1/3 cup of oil and 3 eggs in large bowl with mixer on medium speed or beat vigorously by hand 2 minutes. Pour into pan.
  3. Bake for 26-31 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes before removing from pan.
  4. In a separate pan, melt a stick of butter with another 1/2 cup of orange juice
  5. Poke holes in the cake, then pour melted butter and orange juice over the cake
  6. Dust the cake with powdered sugar
  7. Enjoy

My mom used the make this cake for my birthday every year, and it never lasted more than a day on the counter.
